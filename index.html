Our Story

Traditional recipes since 1985

Founded by the Rossi family in 1985, Gusto Italiano brings the authentic flavors of Italy to your table. Our passion for traditional Italian cooking has been passed down through generations.

We source only the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Italy, to create dishes that capture the essence of Italian cuisine. From our homemade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail.

Our warm and inviting atmosphere makes Gusto Italiano the perfect place for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or celebrations with friends.
